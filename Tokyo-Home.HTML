Tokyo, Japan's bustling capital, blends the ultra-modern with the traditional, from neon-lit skyscrapers to historic temples.
The opulent Meji Shinto Shrine is known for its towering gate and surrounding woods. The Imperial Palace sits amid large public gardens. The city's many museums offer exhhibits ranging from classical art to a reconstructed Kabuki Theatre.

Discover Tokyo

Tokyo boasts a comprehensive and efficient public transportation system, including trains, subways, buses, taxis, and ferries. The most popular and convenient methods for navigating the city are subways and trains, often using prepaid IC cards like Suica or Pasmo. Taxis are readily available and offer excellent service, while buses are a useful option for areas outside the central city.
